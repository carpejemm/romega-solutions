About Us

Who is Romega Solutions

We specialize in identifying and recruiting top-level executives and senior leaders for tech companies. We typically focus on analyst roles such as Chief Analyst, Principal Analyst as well as sales roles such as Chief Sales Officer, VP of Sales, Director of Sales, Account Managers and other senior leadership positions in technology firms. We also connect market research companies that provide insights into the tech industry through market analysis, forecasting, and intelligence typically with the leading Fortune 1000 companies and innovative Start-ups. We specialize with companies that collect and analyze semiconductor data on trends, market size, demand-supply dynamics, and industry growth patterns.
